Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect and use your data.

  • All data is encrypted end-to-end
  • We never sell your personal information
  • You can delete your data at any time
  • Anonymous posting options available
Privacy Policy Details

Data Collection
We collect only essential information: email, username, and safety-related data you choose to share (location during emergencies, incident reports).

Data Usage
Your data is used solely for: providing safety services, improving route recommendations, connecting you with emergency services, and community support features.

Data Protection
We employ industry-standard encryption, secure servers, regular security audits, and strict access controls to protect your information.

Your Rights
You have the right to access, modify, export, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings.

How We Use Your Data

Understanding how your data helps keep you and others safe is important. Here's what we do with the information you share.

  • Location data: Only for real-time safety features
  • Incident reports: Anonymized for community awareness
  • Emergency contacts: Secure storage, only used in SOS
  • Route history: Helps optimize safe path suggestions
Detailed Data Usage

Location Services
We access your location only when you use safety features like safe routes or activate SOS. Location data is encrypted and can be auto-deleted based on your retention settings.

Community Safety Data
Incident reports you share help create safer routes for everyone. All reports are anonymized unless you explicitly choose otherwise.

Emergency Information
Emergency contacts and medical information are stored locally on your device and encrypted in our database, accessible only during SOS activation.

Analytics
We use anonymized usage data to improve app performance and safety features. No personally identifiable information is used in analytics.

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data and account. We respect your rights and make it easy to manage your information.

  • Access and download your data anytime
  • Request data deletion within 48 hours
  • Opt-out of data collection features
  • Change privacy settings at any time
Complete User Rights

Right to Access
You can view all data we have about you through your account dashboard or by requesting a full data export.

Right to Deletion
Request deletion of your account and all associated data. We will process this within 48 hours and confirm when complete.

Right to Rectification
Update or correct any personal information directly through your account settings.

Right to Portability
Download your data in a machine-readable format to transfer to another service if desired.

Right to Object
Opt-out of specific data processing activities while continuing to use core safety features.

Cookie & Tracking Policy

We use minimal cookies necessary for app functionality and your preferences. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

  • Essential cookies for authentication
  • Preference cookies for your settings
  • No advertising or tracking cookies
  • Session data cleared on logout
Cookie Usage Details

Essential Cookies
Required for basic functionality like keeping you logged in, maintaining session security, and remembering your language preference.

Functional Cookies
Store your preferences like theme choice, notification settings, and default privacy options to enhance user experience.

What We Don't Use
We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics that could identify you personally.

Managing Cookies
You can clear cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that essential cookies are required for the app to function properly.
